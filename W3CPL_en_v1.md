# W3CPL v1.0
World Wide Web Comment Policy License

This license provides a reusable, open standard for comment guidelines
intended for websites, forums, social platforms, and online communities
operating on the World Wide Web in a broad and inclusive sense.

By posting comments in a space governed by W3CPL, users are considered
to have agreed to the following rules.

---

## 1. Prohibited Conduct

### 1.1 Abuse and Personal Attacks
The following behaviors are not permitted:
- Insults, harassment, threats, or hostile language
- Derogatory or discriminatory remarks about individuals or groups
- Targeted attempts to demean, intimidate, or shame others

### 1.2 Spam and Disruptive Content
The following actions are prohibited:
- Unsolicited advertisements or promotional posts
- Repetitive or meaningless messages
- Intentional derailment of discussion topics

### 1.3 Trolling and Malicious Provocation
These behaviors are also prohibited:
- Deliberate attempts to create conflict or emotional distress
- Knowingly spreading false information
- Posting solely to provoke negative reactions

### 1.4 Safety and Privacy Violations
The following are strictly forbidden:
- Posting private or identifying information of any individual
- Encouraging harmful or dangerous behavior
- Any conduct that compromises the safety of others

---

## 2. Encouraged Conduct

Users are encouraged to:
- Express opinions with respect and clarity
- Provide constructive criticism or alternatives when raising concerns
- Consider the perspectives and limitations of others
- Contribute positively to the quality of the discussion space

---

## 3. Administrative Authority

The administrator or moderator governing the comment space has full
authority, at their discretion, to:
- Remove or restrict comments
- Limit participation temporarily or permanently
- Interpret and apply this license in context

These actions may be taken without prior notice.

---

## 4. Scope of Application

This license applies to:
- Comment sections
- Replies and threads
- Live chat or similar real-time comment streams
- Any online space where W3CPL is explicitly referenced

"W3CPL v1.0" displayed in a comment space indicates that these rules
govern all user submissions therein.

---

## 5. Updates and Versioning

This license may be updated.  
When an administrator states they use “W3CPL v1.0,” the rules of this
version apply unless otherwise specified.

Future versions will be published at the same repository location.
